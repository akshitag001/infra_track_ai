## 1. Problem Statement

Infrastructure inspection and maintenance in India is largely manual, reactive, and document-heavy. 
Government departments, municipal corporations, and infrastructure companies rely on periodic physical inspections and static PDF/Excel reports. 

This leads to:
- Delayed damage detection
- Fragmented and unstructured data
- Lack of predictive insights
- Increased maintenance costs
- Public safety risks

There is no centralized AI-driven system that converts raw infrastructure data into real-time, visual, and actionable intelligence.

---

## 2. Target Users

- Municipal Corporations
- Smart City Authorities
- Public Works Departments (PWD)
- Construction & EPC Companies
- Infrastructure Monitoring Agencies

---

## 3. Key Pain Points

1. Manual inspection processes
2. Reports stored in PDFs and spreadsheets with no real analytics
3. No automated risk scoring or prioritization
4. No centralized dashboard for decision-makers
5. Reactive maintenance instead of predictive planning

---

## 4. Proposed Solution

InfraTech AI is an AI-powered infrastructure intelligence platform that:

- Ingests raw infrastructure data (images, inspection reports, sensor logs)
- Uses machine learning models to analyze structural conditions
- Generates automated visual dashboards
- Provides risk scoring and predictive maintenance alerts
- Enables data-driven infrastructure decision-making

The system transforms static reports into dynamic, interactive, and predictive infrastructure insights.

---

## 5. Core Features

- Image-based damage detection (cracks, corrosion, structural wear)
- Automated report summarization
- Risk scoring system
- Infrastructure health dashboard
- Trend analysis over time
- Alert system for high-risk assets
